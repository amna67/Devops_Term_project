Frequently Asked Questions

When does the application period open?

Applications are open from January 15 onwards. Submit your application before the deadline on March 31, 2026.

What is the application fee?

The application fee is PKR 2,000. This fee is non-refundable but covers your entrance exam fee.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for up to 3 programs. You'll need to submit separate applications for each program.

What is the scholarship policy?

UCP offers merit-based and need-based scholarships up to 100% of tuition fees for qualifying students.
